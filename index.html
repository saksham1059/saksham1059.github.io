About SDVM

Sanatam Dharam Vidya Mandir (SDVM) is a proud institution of academic excellence, chaotic assemblies, and the occasional goat wandering on the playground. With a legacy older than the school's WiFi router, SDVM remains a beacon of... something.

Our Mission

To shape young minds with strict rules, last-minute notices, and surprise unit tests. At SDVM, we believe in values, traditions, and hiding the AC remote from students.
